Wit Wars - A Thrilling Battle of Wits

  • Wit Wars consists of three mind-bending rounds: Hangman, Categories, and Family Feud.
  • Scheduled for February 19th, 2025, in Tan Audi at 5:30 PM.
  • Students can participate in teams of 3 or 4.

Hangman Round:

  • Two teams face off, guessing a movie/TV show title by filling in the blanks.
  • Teams spin a number wheel, which multiplies points for correct letter guesses.
  • The first team to guess the title correctly earns bonus points.
  • The top 8 teams advance to the next round.

Categories Round:

  • Teams compete by listing items in a given category within 30 seconds.
  • Teams bid on how many they can name; if challenged, they must prove it.
  • Failure to fulfill their claim results in losing the round.
  • Out of 8 teams, only 2 teams shall progress to the final round.

Family Feud Round:

  • Teams buzz in to answer survey-based questions.
  • Correct answers score points.
  • Three wrong answers allow the opposing team to steal points with a correct answer.
  • If the opposing team fails to steal, the first team keeps their points.
  • The team with the most points at the end wins the round.

Additional Guidelines:

  • Unfair means will result in disqualification at the Organizing Committee’s discretion.
