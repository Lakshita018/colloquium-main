RULE BOOK

Strategy-Based Competition

  • A strategy-based competition where teams complete challenges, form alliances, and use specialized cards to attack other teams.
  • Scheduled for February 18th, 2025, at UG Activity Space-2.
  • All teams must be present at the venue by 5:30 PM for entry and preparation.
  • Registration by the team leader is mandatory via the official Colloquium website.
  • Teams may consist of 3 or 4 members.
  • Each team starts with a kingdom and 3000 health points.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Teams use a Task Card to request a challenge from mentors. Completing the challenge awards Attack Points based on difficulty.
  • Failure to complete a challenge results in a 15-minute cooldown before attempting another.
  • Teams can use an Attack Card to attack another team's kingdom by spending Attack Points.
    • Successful attacks reduce the targeted team’s health points and add them to the attacking team's health.
  • The Alliance Card allows two teams to form an alliance, combining their health points.
    • Allied teams can use two Task Cards simultaneously.
  • The Backstab Card is a one-time-use card that allows a team to betray its alliance.
    • When played, the team gains individual Attack Points to use against their alliance partner.
    • This card is played secretly and cannot be revealed to other teams.
  • The Suspicion Card is used to accuse an alliance partner of using a Backstab Card.
    • If the accusation is correct, the betrayer is eliminated.
    • If the accusation is incorrect, the accusing team is eliminated.
  • A team is eliminated if its health points reach zero.
  • The last team with remaining health points is declared the winner.

Additional Guidelines:

  • The Organizing Committee reserves the right to disqualify teams found using unfair means, with the decision being final.
