Flag Heist - Capture the Flag (CTF) Showdown

  • An immersive Capture the Flag (CTF) competition where participants navigate a virtual gaming realm, solve puzzles, and outmaneuver opponents to claim victory.
  • The event consists of two rounds and is scheduled for February 21st at 5:00 PM at TAN Audi.
  • Timings for rounds:
    • Round 1: 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM
    • Round 2: 8:30 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Only participants who qualify in the first round will proceed to the second round.
  • The event is team-based, with each team consisting of 4-5 members.

Registration & Game Setup:

  • The team leader must register on the designated Colloquium portal before the event.
  • The team leader will create a game room on the portal, while other members will join using the provided room code.

Scoring & Leaderboard:

  • Each correctly answered question awards points based on difficulty.
  • A real-time leaderboard will continuously track team scores throughout the event.
  • Final placements and winners will be determined based on the highest accumulated points.

Hints & Gameplay Rules:

  • Hints are available to assist teams, but using them will result in a points deduction to maintain competitiveness.
  • All participants must bring their own fully charged laptops.
  • Teams must ensure a stable network connection for uninterrupted gameplay.
  • Any team found using unfair means or violating event rules will face penalties or immediate disqualification at the discretion of the organizing committee.
